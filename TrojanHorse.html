Trojan Horse

What is a Trojan Horse?

A Trojan horse, often known as a Trojan, is malicious malware or software that appears to be genuine yet has the ability to take control of your computer. A Trojan is a computer program that is meant to hurt, disrupt, steal, or otherwise harm your data or network.

Ways it Affects Computers

Cybercriminals can use Trojans to spy on you, steal your personal information, and obtain backdoor access to your computer.

Symptoms

The first sign that a Trojan virus has infiltrated your computer is when it behaves strangely and the entire machine behaves differently. This behavior includes the mouse moving on, its own programs opening automatically and some applications refusing to start.

Removal

Some Trojans can be removed from your computer by deactivating startup items from untrustworthy sources. To get the best results, start by rebooting your device into safe mode, which will prevent the virus from preventing you from uninstalling it.
